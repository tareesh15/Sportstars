P.A. Vijay

P.A. Vijay (also known as P.A. Vijayakumar or Vijay), is an Indian playback singer, lyricist, and music composer known for his work primarily in Tamil cinema. While he is recognized more for his contributions as a lyricist and composer, he has also made notable contributions as a playback singer. His work is characterized by a mix of traditional and contemporary styles.
