Unnikrishnan

Unnikrishnan (also known as K.S. Unnikrishnan or simply K.S.U), is a renowned Indian playback singer who has made significant contributions to Indian film music. Known for his rich and melodious voice, Unnikrishnan has performed across various Indian languages, including Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, and Hindi.
