Na. Muthukumar

Na. Muthukumar was a celebrated Indian lyricist and poet primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. His contributions to the music industry, particularly in writing evocative and poetic lyrics, earned him widespread acclaim. While Muthukumar was renowned as a lyricist rather than a singer, his work significantly shaped many popular songs in Tamil cinema.
